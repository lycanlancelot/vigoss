FAQ

Common Questions

How does billing work?

You are billed per second of GPU uptime. Your wallet is charged at the end of each session. We accept credit card, PayPal, and bank transfer. There are no setup fees or minimum charges.

Can I bring my own Docker image?

Yes — any public Docker Hub image or private registry image works. We also provide curated images for PyTorch, TensorFlow, Stable Diffusion, and more so you can start immediately.

Where are your data centres?

Our primary data centres are located in Sydney and Melbourne (AU), with nodes in Singapore for Asia-Pacific reach. All data remains within Australian borders by default.

What is Spot pricing?

Spot instances use spare capacity at a steep discount (up to 65% off on-demand). They may be interrupted with 30 seconds notice, making them ideal for fault-tolerant training jobs.

Do you offer reserved / committed pricing?

Yes. Reserve GPUs for 1 month, 3 months, or 1 year and save up to 50% vs on-demand rates. Contact our team for a custom quote.

Is there persistent storage?

Yes. Network volumes are available from 20 GB to 50 TB at AUD $0.07/GB/month. Volumes persist across pod restarts and can be shared between instances.
